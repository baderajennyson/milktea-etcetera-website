About Us

Your New Favorite Hangout Spot

Milktea Etcetera is a cozy and vibrant beverage shop that caters to milk tea lovers and flavor enthusiasts in search of unique drink experiences. More than just your classic bubble tea, it offers a diverse selection of beverages and snacks, blending traditional favorites with creative concoctions. With its inviting atmosphere and budget-friendly treats, Milktea Etcetera is perfect for hangouts, study breaks, and casual indulgence.
